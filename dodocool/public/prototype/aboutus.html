  • About MFI

    MFi certification, also called Apple MFi certification, is one logo use license that the United States Apple's Company grants to Apple outside accessories and peripheral products (eg: Interface, Power Supply, wire and audio and so on) produced by Apple authorized accessory manufactures. The MFI certificate, authorized by Apple on a gloabal scale, is actually short of "made for iPod","made for iPhone","made for iPad".

  • MFI Signification

    MFI Authorized products have to subjected to multiple tests before get a certified license, and its pass rate is only 2% in the worldwide. If one enterprise want to get the MFI certificate, not only its Product brand need to get through MFi certification, but each and every one of its product model has to pass it. Products With MFi certificate can provide more reliable quality assurance in terms of product design, quality, compatibility. One enterprise can successfully get MFi license also represents its strong comprehensive strength.
